### Step-by-Step Data Acquisition



**Method**: Web Interface Download

To reproduce this exact dataset, follow these steps:

1. Navigate to USDA NASS QuickStats: Go to: https://quickstats.nass.usda.gov/
2. Apply Filters (in order): 

* Sector: Select: CROPS
* Group: Select: FIELD CROPS
* Commodity: Select: CORN
* Category: Select ALL of the following:

&nbsp;	\* AREA PLANTED

&nbsp;	\* AREA HARVESTED

&nbsp;	\* PRODUCTION

&nbsp;	\* YIELD

* Geographic Level: Select: STATE
* State: Select: ILLINOIS
* Year: Enter range: 1902 to 2025

3\. Execute Query: Click "Spreadsheet" button, your dataset will download as a CSV

4\. Save as: nass\_qs\_1902\_to\_2025.csv (or your preferred filename)
